Watergate Salad

Description

A classic recipe full of nostalgia. A great side dish or dessert with pistachio, pineapple, marshmallow and whipped topping. Discover why so many have enjoyed this weird dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 package pistachio pudding mix.
  • 1 can crushed pineapple.
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows.
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts.
  • 4 ounces frozen whipped topping, thawed.
  • Crushed pistachios.
  • Maraschino cherries.

Steps

  1. Combine crushed pineapple with juice, instant pudding mix, marshmallows and walnuts in large bowl and mix.
  2. Fold in whipped topping. Chill for 1 to 2 hours.
  3. Garnish with crushed pistachios and maraschino cherries.
  4. Serve.
