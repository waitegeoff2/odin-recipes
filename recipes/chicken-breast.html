Mediterranean Chicken Breast

Description

Here's a tasty, simple chicken breast recipe using mediterranean flavors including lemon juice, olive oil, and herbs. All baked in the oven!

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper.
  • 1/4 cup olive oil.
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice.
  • 1 clove garlic, minced.
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano, or more.
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme, or to taste.

Steps

  1. Season with salt and pepper and place in a bowl. Combine olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano and thyme in a small bowl and pour marinade. Marinate for 10 minutes at room temp.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 F (200 C). Set oven rack about 6 inches from heat.
  3. Place chickens onto a baking dish and pour marinade on top.
  4. Bake chicken on middle rack until no longer pink in the middle and juices run clear. Roughly 35 to 45 minutes. Move baking dish to top rack and broil for roughly 5 minutes, until browned.
