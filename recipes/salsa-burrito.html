Salsa Chicken Burrito Filling

Description

Easy, delicious filling for burritos or tacos. Freezes great! To make into burrito, simply fill tortillas, put them in a baking dish, cover with salsa and cheese and bake for 10 minutes. Nice!

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves.
  • 1 can tomato sauce.
  • 1/4 cup salsa.
  • 1 package taco seasoning mix.
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin.
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced.
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder.
  • Hot sauce to taste.

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts and tomato sauce in medium saucepan over high heat. Bring to boil, then add salsa, seasoning, cumin, garlic and chili powder. Let simmer for 15 minutes.
  2. With a fork, pull apart chicken into thin strings. Keep cooking pulled chicken for 5 to 10 minutes. If gets too thick, just add some water.
  3. You're done!
